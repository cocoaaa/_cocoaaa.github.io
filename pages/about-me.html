About Me

I'm a budding web developer, trying to figure out my place in the planet Earth and in the place of vast Web. I like wiggling around and twisting little bits and pieces to understand a system, let it be a computer, a program or a life. Not always successful, but relatively positive and resilient in face of failure. Learning to grow up, be more responsible, and appreciate what I have. I aim to live a day for something greater than myself, wondering always whether I can someday find someone/some place more important than myself. Let that be my love(r), passion, or purpose, I will be a little bit more like a grownup when I find it.

This blog is a way for me to organize my thoughts and learnings and to share them with others so that they truely have meanings. By setting a foot into a wider and wilder Web, I might hope to feel more of part of a bigger community.

I believe everything starts small. Although being obsessed with staying on the right tract all the time may distract you from focusing on your actions, I hope being consicous of the danger of falling into loopholes nevertheless hurts.

May this blog be the start of my brave adventure to jump into the fathomless rabbit hole with big eyes to face the invisible, and thus often time fearful futures.

May this be another start of my little journey to become a big adult.
